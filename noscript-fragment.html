EthUX — Ethereum UX Pain Points, Solutions & Research

A community-sourced map of 32 Ethereum UX pain points with real user evidence, solution checklists, and adoption tracking. Built from 32,000+ community reports.

UX Problem Categories

  • User Onboarding — Make the first 5 minutes effortless. 5 problems including the gas hurdle, english-only recovery phrases, forced backup friction.
  • Transaction Clarity — Help users understand every transaction. 7 problems including blind signing, signing fatigue, missing signing context.
  • Cross-chain Flow — Make moving between chains invisible. 4 problems including single-chain balance display, manual network switching, bridging pain.
  • Safety & Security — Build trust through transparency and user control. 3 problems including prevalence of scams, key management burden, spam & junk tokens.
  • Mobile & Connectivity — Bring wallet connection up to mobile standards. 3 problems including connection failures, mobile connection dance, mobile apps don't respond.
  • Accessibility — Open the door for 75% of non-English speakers. 3 problems including jargon overload, assumed user knowledge, poor localization.
  • Protocol Design — Unlock better UX at the protocol layer. 2 problems including no default native account abstraction, on-chain activity is public by default.
  • Daily Operations — Polish the flows people use every day. 5 problems including unpredictable gas fees, fiat-based values missing, no portfolio & tax tracking.
